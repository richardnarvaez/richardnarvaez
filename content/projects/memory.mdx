### Paltaforma de aprendizaje

Memory ClickStudio (MCS) is a digital learning platform for all ages that will help you improve information
retention and speed reading.

### NoteBooks

The users can have multiple notebooks, allowing them to separate each one for the topics they want
to study or review. Additionally, they have the opportunity to study directly from public books
prepared especially by teachers.
